Te Whare Tapa Wha

My Wellbeing Plan

Taha Wairua

Practice mindfulness and gratitude daily
Help others when I have the chance
Stop and enjoy moments of wonder at nature

Taha Hinengaro

Allow myself some downtime for me for at least 30mins a day where I do whatever I feel like
Take myself outside to breath when feeling overwhelmed or challenged

Taha Tinana

Feed myself and my whanau delicious and nutritious food daily(before 2pm for me!)
Get to bed before 11pm at least twice a week!
Take the time to appreciate the body I have!

Taha Whanau

Ensure I take time out to play games or chat with my girls daily
Take the time to connect in with a friend weekly
take time to appreciate (and accept) help offered by my support networks!

Whenua

Have my hot drink on the deck or back step to make sure have some time relaxing and enjoying the outdoors each day
Continue to appreciate the world around us on bike rides to activities
