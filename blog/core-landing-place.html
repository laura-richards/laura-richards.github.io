You found my Core blogs

Core: Core development means relating to human or 'soft' skills rather than technical skills. Among other things, human skills are those we use to take care of ourselves and others, to plan out our studies, to communicate and work well with others. By developing these skills we can be not only the best version of ourselves and get the most out of our time, but we can be better friends, colleagues and create healthier environments around us.
